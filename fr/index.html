C'KADO

Gift voucher sales platform for small merchant, you can find merchant by name adress or city and with geolocation. Generation of pdf and sending of mail automatically. Dashboard for merchants to see sales, invoices, statistics and promote their sales module on social networks. Developed with MVC pattern: framework Slim 2.0 and Eloquent, payement method with Braintree (Paypal compagny) and MangoPay. Currently c-kado.fr is online and work very well.

Articom, 2016 HTML 5, CSS, jQuery, PHP, MySql

SportNet

SportNet is a school project developed by group of four, it's a platform to manage sport event, There are two types of users: the participants and the organizer.
